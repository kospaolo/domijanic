Villa Nika

Family Domijanic has been in tourism for two generations. House Nika is built with lots of love and are completely equipped with exterior and interior style. Two generations of tourism experiences have taught us that every guest is important. We are at our guests 24 hours a day, we look forward to staying in this business and in the coming decades and continue to offer quality and comfortable accommodation to our guests.

This holiday home with swimming pool is located on the eastern coast of Istria, near the small town of Labin. It is functionally furnished and is a perfect choice for family holiday with children. Ten minutes of drive separates you from the most beautiful beaches in Istria – the beaches in Rabac.

This holiday home will provide guests with 3 bedrooms, a TV and air conditioning. The property also has 3 bathrooms with a shower and and outdoor pool.

Ten minutes of drive separates you from the most beautiful beaches in Istria – the beaches in Rabac. During your stay, you will surely enjoy the peace and tranquillity of the rural ambience.The whole of Istria is an ideal holiday destination that arouses the soul. Discover the beauty of numerous small villages with just a few stone houses, Romanesque churches painted with frescoes, medieval castles or prehistoric castles on the hillsides and all this immersed in the greenery of the beauty of nature in the Mediterranean.
