DESTINATION: ISTRA


The whole of Istria is an ideal holiday destination that arouses the soul. Discover the beauty of numerous small villages with just a few stone houses, Romanesque churches painted with frescoes, medieval castles or prehistoric castles on the hillsides and all this immersed in the greenery of the beauty of nature in the Mediterranean. And again, whenever you want, you can experience a dynamic rhythm and a rich offer of gastronomy, entertainment, sports facilities and attractions that offer touristic places on the coast.


DESTINATION: LABIN


On the hill above Rapca lies the medieval town of Labin, which was first mentioned as Albona in 285 AD. It is the birthplace of Matija Vlačić, a reformer and associate of Martin Luther, where numerous cultural and historical monuments that bear witness to the rich history of the city transform into the ateliers, the refugees of numerous artists, painters and sculptors, as well as the local youth who are glad to gather there in numerous small , charming locations.


DESTINATION: RABAC


Crystal sea and beaches. The east coast of Istria is known for its crystal clear sea of top quality and purity of water. Beaches in the Rabac tourist resort are very well equipped with lounges, umbrellas and sports equipment and sports facilities. The beaches in Rabac are characterized by the Blue Flag - a sign that guarantees the ultimate cleanliness of the sea and the beauty of the beaches.


GASTRONOMY


Istria is already a well-known gastro region, home to many award-winning top restaurants, but also excellent domestic taverns where you can enjoy local food and indigenous groceries. Discover Istrian cuisine and its meals that alternate in the seasons, always offering fresh foods and fruits of nature. Season with aromatic herbs, premium extra virgin olive oil and top quality Istrian winemakers.


TREKKING


The eastern coast of Istria is rocky and is situated at a higher altitude that provides a special connection between the sea and the mountains and offers an enchanting view of the sea and Kvarner Bay. To the north of Labin, there are two mountains of Učka and Čičarija, where you can enjoy hiking, trekking and even hiking.
