Villa Maris

Set in settlement Pustić near Labin, Holiday home Maris offers accommodation with free WiFi, air conditioning, a restaurant and access to a garden with an outdoor pool. Featuring free private parking, the holiday home is in an area where guests can engage in activities such as hiking, windsurfing and cycling

The holiday home features 3 bedrooms, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, an equipped kitchen with a dishwasher and a microwave, a washing machine, and 2 bathrooms with a shower

The holiday home offers a fitness centre. A barbecue can be found at Holiday home Maris, along with a shared lounge

Pula is 47 km from the accommodation, while Rabac is 9 km away. The nearest airport is Pula Airport, 42 km from Holiday home Maris
