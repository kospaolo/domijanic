Villa Maris

This beautiful holiday home with pool is located on the eastern coast of Istria, in the village Snašići near Labin. It is modernly furnished and has 3 bedrooms, 3 bathrooms, an additional toilet, and a kitchen with dining area and living room. The refreshment will provide you with a nice outdoor pool, while you can spend your evenings in socializing by tasting your favorite barbecued dishes

During your stay, visit the nearby old town of Labin, the venue for numerous summer festivals. Do not miss the beautiful pebble beaches and crystal clear sea in a tourist resort Rabac with sport recreation facilities and a beautiful promenade along the sea. You can also witness in Pula a rich cultural and entertainment program, especially during the time of the famous Pula Film Festival.

Pula is 47 km from the accommodation, while Rabac is 9 km away. The nearest airport is Pula Airport, 42 km from Holiday home Maris
